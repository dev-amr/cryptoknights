Transactions

Bitcoins and other digital currencies can be purchased/exchanged for fiat currencies like the British Pound, US Dollar, Euros and many others in many different ways. Some ways are of course safer than others.

Is as easy as scanning the QR code or entering the address of whoever you want to send bitcoins to, enter the amount, check everything is correct and hit send. Receiving bitcoins into your wallet is just as easy. As easy as sending an email from your private email address. log in – decide what you want to send and to who – press send.

So what now? Go out and spend your Bitcoins in a coffee shop, on a flight to a sunny destination, hold as an investment, transfer funds to your friends or loved ones abroad or right next to you. The choice is entirely yours.
